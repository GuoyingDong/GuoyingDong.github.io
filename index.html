Research Interest

1. Functionally Graded Materials

Functionally graded materials have been widely used in many applications with the development of Additive Manufacturing. How to design the functionally graded materials is still a challenging issue. Simulation-based design is a popular way. The main contribution is that I provided design and simulation tool for researchers to design functionally graded materials manufactured by AM.

The application includes aerospace lightweight structure design, cellular medical implant design, and conformal cooling channel design. My homogenization code has been downloaded more than 190 times on MATLAB community website. It has been widely used to estimate the mechanical property of cellular materials. The hybrid element model I proposed is also adopted by some companies to predict the mechanical performance of solid lattice hybrid structures.

2. Customized Insole Design

My current research in SUTD is in collaboration with RESA Wearables Inc. in the USA.

This project is to design an AM fabricated customized insole with functionally graded infill patterns. The mechanical property of the insole is adjustable by controlling the infill pattern in the process planning stage. A simulation model will be proposed to predict the mechanical property of the internal structure. The process parameters will be optimized to improve the fabrication speed for mass production.

I am also working on the CAD for AM project in SUTD. The objective is to incorporate the manufacturing data into the design and simulation process to optimize the product performance.
